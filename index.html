Who is JS?

My name is Jon Salamat. I am a freelance Graphic Designer with strong Photoshop and Illustration skills and am looking to expand into Web Development and Multi-media design. I am proficient in all common Graphic Softwares such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, Quark, Dreamweaver, and Flash. I have strong essential knowledge of Graphic Design Compositions, such as Typograhic Design, Grid Design, and various layout compositions for spreads, ads, etc. Also, I am a very talented illustrator and a versatile photographer. In addition to Graphic Design, I am open to job positions in illustration and sequential arts.
